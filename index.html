WHAT IS DABIRISM?

Dabirism is an absolute dictatorship ideology created and ruled by Noah Dabir, in which the goal is to make the entire world 100% controlled by him. Under Dabirism, every government, institution, and system is dismantled and rebuilt under Noah’s command. There is no president, no parliament, no opposing party—only Noah Dabir, the supreme leader of humanity.

At the heart of Dabirism is a promise: Noah Dabir vows to always make good decisions for the future of the world. Every action he takes is with the goal of bringing peace, order, strength, and greatness to mankind. promised that all of the people who joined dabirism will have true freedom, peace, protection, and loyalty by the state.
